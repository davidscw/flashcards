bodyZh:
  - <strong>1. 每日一卡</strong>——每天選一張卡片。早上讀一遍，留意這個概念在你一天當中會怎麼出現。晚上簡單回想：「今天我看到了什麼？我學到了什麼？」
  - <strong>2. 跟家人一起聊</strong>——一起讀一張卡片，問一些開放式的問題：對小孩子：「你的身體什麼時候覺得安全？」對青少年：「你壓力大的時候，身體有什麼感覺？」對成年子女：「關於自己的身體，你還在學什麼？」
  - <strong>3. 寫日記</strong>——把卡片當作日記的題目。例如卡片3（身體會記住一切）：「小時候，我的身體學到了什麼關於安全的事？」卡片44（耗竭是身體的警報）：「在我快要耗竭之前，身體給我哪些訊號？」
  - <strong>4. 在小組裡使用</strong>——在父母成長團體、讀書會、支持團體裡使用這些卡片。一起讀一張卡片，然後分享：哪一點讓你有感覺？哪一點你覺得困難？你想試試看什麼？
bodyEn:
  - <strong>1. Daily Practice</strong> — Choose one card each morning. Notice the concept during your day. Reflect briefly each evening.
  - "<strong>2. Family Conversations</strong> — Read a card together. Ask: \"When does your body feel safe? What helps when it doesn't?\""
  - "<strong>3. Reflective Journaling</strong> — Use cards as prompts. Example: Card 3: \"What did my body learn about safety when I was young?\""
  - <strong>4. Group Use</strong> — In parent groups or support circles, read a card and share what resonates, challenges, or you want to try.
  - <strong>5. Therapeutic Support</strong> — Therapists can use cards for shared vocabulary, psychoeducation, and home practice.
  - <strong>6. Self-Paced Learning</strong> — Work through all 64 cards over 64 days, or take one part at a time.
